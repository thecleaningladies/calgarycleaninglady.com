Preparing for Your Cleaning Service

To help us provide the best cleaning experience, we recommend:

  • Tidy up personal items and clutter before we arrive
  • Remove all items from floors (shoes, toys, baskets, etc.)
  • Clear surfaces of valuables and fragile items
  • Secure pets in a separate area during cleaning
  • Communicate any special requirements or areas of focus

This preparation helps our team focus on deep cleaning rather than organizing, ensuring you get the most value from our service!

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Calgary do you serve?

We serve all areas of Calgary and surrounding communities including Airdrie, Cochrane, and Chestermere. Our service area extends within a 30km radius of downtown Calgary.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide us with keys or access codes. All our cleaners are fully bonded and insured for your peace of mind.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use hospital-grade, eco-friendly cleaning products that are effective yet safe for children and pets. We can also use your preferred products upon request.

How do I prepare for a deep cleaning service?

For best results, we recommend:

  • Tidying up personal items and clutter
  • Removing all items from floors (toys, shoes, etc.)
  • Clearing surfaces of valuables and fragile items
  • Securing pets in a separate area
  • Communicating any special requirements

Are your cleaners background checked?

Yes, all our cleaners undergo thorough background checks, reference verification, and receive comprehensive training before joining our team.
